# Privacy Policy - Corevia

## EN
Our Discord bot respects user privacy and collects only the necessary data to provide its features. 
We do not store messages or personal information outside Discord. 
User data such as member status, roles, and activity are used temporarily in memory for real-time processing only. 
Users can opt-out of data tracking at any time by adjusting bot settings. 
Message content is never used for AI training or analytics. 
We follow Discord's Terms of Service and Privacy Guidelines.
